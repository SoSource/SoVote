Why not Apple?

Let me tell you about Apple.

Apple charges an annual subscription to develop on their platform, not distribute, develop. After needing to buy a newish MacBook and iPhone of course. Then if you distribute and make any money Apple takes 30%, forever. What I live on for a full year Apple makes every 7 seconds, yet they do this to every small indie developer. Google does not.

So even despite this I decided to make an app, I learned a new language and spent a lot of time building something I was reasonably happy with, you can get it for free in the above stores. Apple however, rejected it. They made numerous major demands for changes which would have given me significantly more development and maintenance and resulted in something I wasn't happy with. It was too far for me. At this time, maintaining an Apple app store presence is not in my means.

Things like this happen because people give thousands of dollars to a trillion dollar, authoritarian corporation when there are alternatives available.
